Mahatma Gandhi

A skinny man with the courage of a lion and simplicity of a Monk

Timeline of his work and Philosophy

  • Before 1915- He was actively fighting against the Britani law which discriminated blacks of South Africa
  • He actively participated in freedom struggle alongside Nelson Mandela in South Africa
  • He returned to India in 1915, and joined the farmers movement with the Congress(Naram dal)
  • 1916- He led the farmers movement in Champaran Bihar against the 90 percent tax collection by the British.
  • 1917- He won the case against them allowing farmers to pay just 20 percent of their generated indigo plants
